Immersive Geo Challenge

A GeoGuessr-style classroom game for the Immersive Room, using 360° panorama images instead of Google Street View. Choose a difficulty level before starting.

Choose Level 1, 2 or 3 Up to 5 rounds 90 seconds per round Team friendly GitHub Pages ready
  • Look carefully around the 360° scene.
  • Discuss clues with your team.
  • Tap the map to place your guess.
  • The closer your guess, the higher your score.
Teacher setup: add your panorama images to an images folder and update the LOCATIONS list inside this HTML file. The starter locations below use placeholder filenames so you can add your own properly licensed images.
Level 1

Easy

Recognisable landmarks and clearer visual clues. Best for warm-ups, ESOL learners or first-time players.

Level 2

Medium

Famous places, but learners need to pay closer attention to signs, buildings, landscape and context.

Level 3

Hard

More challenging global locations. Learners need to use subtle clues and discuss their reasoning.
